# Acme Corp Onboarding Guide

## First Day Checklist
New hires should report to the main reception at 9:00 AM on their first day. Bring
a government-issued photo ID and completed I-9 form. IT will provide a company laptop
(MacBook Pro or Dell XPS, based on department allocation) pre-configured with required
software. Badge access is provisioned within 2 business days.

## Required Training
All new employees must complete the following training within their first 30 days:
1. Security Awareness Training (2 hours) — covers phishing, password policy, and
   data classification.
2. Anti-Harassment and Inclusion Training (1.5 hours) — mandatory for all roles.
3. Compliance Basics (1 hour) — overview of SOX, GDPR, and industry regulations.
4. Tool Orientation (varies) — Slack, Jira, Confluence, GitHub, and department-specific tools.

Failure to complete required training within 30 days results in a hold on system access
until completion.

## Probation Period
The standard probation period is 90 days. During probation, employees receive
bi-weekly check-ins with their manager. At the end of probation, a formal assessment
determines confirmation of employment. Either party may terminate the employment
relationship during probation with 1 week's notice.

## Benefits Enrollment
Health, dental, and vision insurance enrollment must be completed within 15 days
of the start date. The company covers 90% of individual premiums and 70% of
dependent premiums. 401(k) matching begins after 6 months of employment at a
rate of 100% match on the first 4% of salary contributed.

## Buddy Program
Each new hire is paired with a buddy from their team for the first 60 days. Buddies
are expected to schedule at least two 30-minute check-ins per week and help the new
hire navigate team norms, tools, and processes.
