# Acme Corp Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP sign-off and a quarterly review.
Remote employees must be available during core hours (10 AM to 4 PM local time)
and maintain a stable internet connection of at least 50 Mbps.

## Leave Policy
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accruing at
1.67 days per month. PTO does not roll over beyond 5 days into the next calendar
year. Sick leave is provided separately at 10 days per year and does not require
advance notice. Parental leave is 16 weeks fully paid for the primary caregiver
and 6 weeks for the secondary caregiver.

## Expense Policy
Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to $75 per day. Receipts are
required for any single expense over $25. Submit expenses within 30 days of the
trip. Late submissions may be denied. Flight bookings must use economy class for
domestic travel; business class is permitted for international flights over 6 hours.

## Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually in June and December. Each review
includes a self-assessment, a manager evaluation, and a calibration session. Ratings
use a five-point scale: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Developing, Needs
Improvement, and Unsatisfactory. Promotion eligibility requires a minimum of two
consecutive "Meets Expectations" or higher ratings.

## Code of Conduct
All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct, which prohibits
harassment, discrimination, fraud, and conflicts of interest. Violations should be
reported to HR or through the anonymous ethics hotline at 1-800-555-ETHX. Retaliation
against employees who report in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## Data Retention
Customer records are retained for 7 years after account closure, after which
they are permanently deleted in accordance with the data protection policy.
Internal employee records are retained for 3 years after separation from the company.
