# Acme Corp Q1 2026 Financial Summary

## Revenue Overview
Total revenue for Q1 2026 was $47.3 million, representing a 12% year-over-year
increase from $42.2 million in Q1 2025. Subscription revenue accounted for 78%
of total revenue at $36.9 million. Professional services contributed $7.1 million
and one-time license fees contributed $3.3 million.

## Key Metrics
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: $152 million (up from $138 million in Q4 2025)
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: 118%
- **Gross Margin**: 72.4% (up from 70.1% in Q1 2025)
- **Customer Count**: 1,247 active accounts (net addition of 89 in Q1)
- **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: $121,800 (up 8% year-over-year)
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Payback**: 14 months

## Expenses
Total operating expenses for Q1 2026 were $41.8 million:
- **Cost of Revenue**: $13.0 million (27.6% of revenue)
- **Research & Development**: $12.4 million (26.2% of revenue)
- **Sales & Marketing**: $10.2 million (21.6% of revenue)
- **General & Administrative**: $6.2 million (13.1% of revenue)

## Profitability
- **Operating Income**: $5.5 million (11.6% operating margin)
- **Net Income**: $4.2 million (8.9% net margin)
- **EBITDA**: $8.1 million (17.1% EBITDA margin)
- **Free Cash Flow**: $6.8 million

## Headcount
Total headcount at the end of Q1 2026: 623 employees
- Engineering: 248 (40%)
- Sales & Marketing: 187 (30%)
- G&A (including HR, Finance, Legal): 112 (18%)
- Customer Success: 76 (12%)

Net hiring in Q1: 34 new hires. Attrition rate: 3.2% (annualized 12.8%).

## Guidance
Management reaffirms full-year 2026 revenue guidance of $200–$210 million,
representing 15–20% year-over-year growth. R&D investment is expected to
increase to 28% of revenue in Q2 to support the new AI product line launch.

## Risks
Key risks identified for the upcoming quarters:
1. Macroeconomic uncertainty may slow enterprise deal closures.
2. Increased competition in the mid-market segment from two new entrants.
3. Foreign exchange headwinds affecting EMEA revenue (approximately 22% of total).
