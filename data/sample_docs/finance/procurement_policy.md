# Acme Corp Procurement Policy

## Purpose
This policy governs the purchase of goods, services, and software by Acme Corp
employees. All purchases must comply with this policy to ensure fiscal responsibility,
competitive pricing, and proper authorization.

## Authorization Thresholds
| Purchase Amount | Required Approver |
|---|---|
| Up to $5,000 | Manager (direct supervisor) |
| $5,001 – $25,000 | Director level |
| $25,001 – $100,000 | VP level + Finance review |
| $100,001 – $500,000 | CFO approval |
| Over $500,000 | CFO + CEO approval; Board notification |

Splitting purchases to circumvent approval thresholds is prohibited and may result
in disciplinary action.

## Purchase Order (PO) Process
1. Requestor submits a purchase requisition in the procurement system (Coupa)
   with a business justification, budget code, and vendor quote.
2. Approvals route based on the authorization thresholds above.
3. Upon approval, a PO is automatically generated and sent to the vendor.
4. Goods/services must be receipted in Coupa within 5 business days of delivery.
5. Three-way matching (PO, receipt, invoice) is required before payment is released.

## Preferred Vendors
Acme Corp maintains a list of preferred vendors negotiated by the procurement team.
Employees should use preferred vendors whenever possible to leverage pre-negotiated
rates. Using a non-preferred vendor for purchases over $10,000 requires a written
justification explaining why the preferred vendor cannot meet the requirement.

## Software Purchases
All software purchases and SaaS subscriptions must be reviewed by IT Security
before approval, regardless of amount. This includes free-tier products that
process company data. Shadow IT (unapproved software) is prohibited. The annual
software review in Q1 evaluates all active subscriptions for continued need and
cost optimization.

## Travel and Entertainment
Business travel must be booked through the company's travel management tool (Concur).
Domestic flights must be economy class. International flights over 6 hours may
be business class with VP approval. Hotel stays should not exceed the GSA per-diem
rate for the destination city. Client entertainment expenses require pre-approval
for amounts over $200.

## Budget Management
Each department is allocated an annual procurement budget during the Q4 planning
cycle. Budget owners are responsible for tracking spend against allocation.
Overspending by more than 10% of the quarterly allocation requires a variance
explanation to Finance. Unspent budget does not carry over to the next fiscal year.

## Exceptions
Exceptions to this policy require written approval from the CFO. Emergency
purchases (e.g., critical infrastructure failure) may be made without a PO
but must be documented within 48 hours with a retroactive requisition.
