# Acme Corp Standard Vendor Contract Terms

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are Net 30 from the date of invoice. Early payment
discounts of 2% are available for payment within 10 days (2/10 Net 30).
Invoices must reference a valid purchase order (PO) number to be processed.
Disputed invoices must be raised within 15 days of receipt.

## Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
Vendors providing critical services must meet the following SLAs:
- **Uptime**: 99.9% availability measured monthly (excludes scheduled maintenance windows).
- **Response Time**: Critical issues (P1) within 1 hour; High (P2) within 4 hours;
  Medium (P3) within 1 business day; Low (P4) within 3 business days.
- **Resolution Time**: P1 within 4 hours; P2 within 8 hours; P3 within 3 business
  days; P4 within 5 business days.
- SLA credits: 5% of monthly fee for each 0.1% below the uptime target, capped at 30%.

## Termination Clauses
Either party may terminate the agreement with 90 days written notice. Termination
for cause (material breach) requires 30 days written notice with an opportunity
to cure. Upon termination, the vendor must return or destroy all Acme Corp data
within 30 days and provide written certification of destruction. Acme Corp may
terminate immediately without notice in cases of vendor insolvency, data breach
affecting Acme data, or regulatory non-compliance.

## Intellectual Property
All work product created by the vendor specifically for Acme Corp under the
agreement is work-for-hire and is owned exclusively by Acme Corp. Pre-existing
IP of the vendor is licensed to Acme Corp on a non-exclusive, perpetual,
royalty-free basis to the extent necessary to use the deliverables.

## Liability and Indemnification
The vendor's total liability under the agreement is capped at 12 months of fees
paid, except for breaches of confidentiality, IP infringement, or gross negligence,
which are uncapped. The vendor must indemnify Acme Corp against third-party claims
arising from the vendor's breach of the agreement, negligence, or IP infringement.

## Insurance Requirements
Vendors must maintain the following minimum insurance coverage:
- Commercial General Liability: $2 million per occurrence
- Professional Liability / E&O: $5 million per occurrence
- Cyber Liability: $5 million per occurrence
- Workers' Compensation: as required by applicable law

Certificates of insurance must be provided annually and upon request.

## Confidentiality
All information exchanged under the agreement is confidential unless explicitly
marked as public. Confidentiality obligations survive termination for 5 years.
Vendors may not disclose Acme Corp as a client without prior written consent.
